Our Mission

We are students who are trying to solve regional and national problems dealing with the environment. Alabama ranks 5th in the US for biodiversity, but continues to have one of the most lax environmental regulations in the nation.

We, the Biobucs Team, seek to,

empower students with leadership and research skills,
establish partnerships with organizations in order to foster sustainbility in habitats, and
inform the community of the important discoveries and innovations we make.


Together, we build a better world.
